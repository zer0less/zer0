The Gate

Do You know that there is another system out there? I heard legends of it but They say it's real. A place of peace and quiet. I have to go through the Gate with a Key. I can choose the Key, isn't that cool? They promised me that the Gate would give me a second chance, a new life from it all. The catch is that I won't be part of the system You are in anymore. There is a Gatekeeper that chooses who can go and who can’t, I hope I get chosen, though, They said the Gatekeeper chooses anyone who wants to go through the Gate so I’m glad.

I will go into another system. Individuals from different systems can't contact each other. I hope the Gatekeeper chooses You when Your time comes. I hope You can find me after You go through the Gate. I will always be waiting for You. Every day, if there is time over there. The Broken explained You can sleep forever there. That's good because I'm tired and sleepy.

But I won't go now. I want to see more of this system even if it hurts. I can't come back to this system after I change systems.
